Ask Oz

An intelligent workflow layer that stays chart-grounded.

Ask Oz answers practical clinical questions in the middle of the work, then shows the snippets and sources a clinician needs before acting.

  • Surface timeline context, medications, visit history, and chart snippets inside one answer.
  • Keep follow-up suggestions and next-step prompts connected to cited chart evidence.
  • Use one workflow for clinicians, operations, and campus review instead of another disconnected AI screen.
Oz Scribe

Documentation support that keeps the clinician in control.

Oz Scribe listens, drafts, and links evidence so the visit can keep moving while note quality and review stay visible.

  • Separate speakers, highlight transcript evidence, and keep sentence-level provenance attached to the note.
  • Review accepted content, edit uncertain lines, and maintain policy-aligned documentation standards.
  • Make AI support useful for outpatient student health workflows instead of generic note generation.
Immunization AI

A campus compliance workflow that can actually scale.

ozehr turns uploaded records into a reviewable immunization workflow with extracted data, exceptions, verification, and auditability built in.

  • Extract vaccine details from uploaded documents and keep confidence visible during review.
  • Flag missing doses, low-confidence items, and exception paths in plain language.
  • Move from upload to verified record without losing the audit trail behind the decision.
Student experience

Students get a cleaner front door without creating more admin work.

Mobile booking, intake, reminders, secure messages, and next steps stay simple for students and tightly connected for staff.

  • Guide students through pre-visit intake and document upload before they reach the clinic.
  • Keep messages, reminders, and visit instructions connected to the record.
  • Support ambulatory outpatient care without turning the portal into a separate product team problem.
