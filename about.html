About ozehr

A college health EHR built around the way campus care works.

ozehr brings medical care, counseling, immunizations, student access, operations, and built-in AI into one connected system for colleges and universities.

ozehr is operated by ozehr LLC, a Nevada limited liability company. Public business mailing address: 732 S 6th St, Ste N, Las Vegas, NV 89101.

Built as if the EHR were invented today

ozehr is cloud-hosted, modern, responsive, and built around the workflows we would design if the EHR were invented today.

AI that reduces clicks and friction

We use AI thoughtfully to reduce clicks, surface context, support documentation, and ingest records, while keeping clinicians in control of review and decision-making.

A deliberately simple business model

We do not nickel-and-dime essential capabilities or ask health centers to decide whether the best workflow is worth an extra charge.

What we care about
We build integrated features for the whole platform and keep our commitments as the technology evolves.
Care teams should be able to use the best workflow without wondering whether it belongs to another tier.
