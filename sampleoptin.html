SMS consent workflow

Patients may opt in to ozehr SMS during account setup or from Communication preferences. SMS consent is optional, the checkbox is unchecked by default, and patients can opt out any time by replying STOP or using the app.

Opt out Reply STOP or update Communication preferences

How patients opt in

1. Patient opens the form

The SMS option is shown during account setup and in Communication preferences.

2. Checkbox starts unchecked

The patient must actively select the checkbox. Account creation and care do not require SMS consent.

3. Consent terms are visible

The form states message types, frequency, rates, STOP, HELP, Terms, Privacy, and support information.

Opt-in disclosure

By checking this box, I consent to receive recurring SMS text messages from ozehr, operated by ozehr LLC, at the mobile number I provided. Messages may include appointment reminders and confirmations, lab result availability notices, care alerts, and account notifications. Message frequency varies, up to 5 messages per week. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help. Consent is not required to receive care or create an account. Terms: https://ozehr.com/terms Privacy: https://ozehr.com/privacy

Opt-out and privacy

STOP

Patients can reply STOP to unsubscribe from future SMS messages.

HELP

Patients can reply HELP for support information, or contact contact@ozehr.com.

No marketing SMS

Messages are limited to transactional healthcare and account notifications. SMS opt-in data is not sold or shared for marketing.
